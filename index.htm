Locating Your Subscriber/Member ID

Your Subscriber/Member ID contains both letters and numbers. It is located in the upper left of your BCBSNC Member ID Card under the name of the primary account holder.

If required, your Member Code is the 2-digit number to the right of a member's name. It may appear at the end of your ID on some documents and/or Medicare cards, but it is typically not needed when completing forms online.
